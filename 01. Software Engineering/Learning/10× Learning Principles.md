	## 1. Focus on Fundamentals  
  
- Master the basics before advanced topics.  
  
---  
  
## 2. Set Deadlines  
  
- [ ] Give every learning goal a deadline.  
- [ ] Avoid learning without a timeline.  
  
---  
  
## 3. Slow Down Content Consumption  
  
Don't rush through tutorials.  
  
Instead:  
  
- Understand  
- Practice  
- Apply  
- Revise  
  
---  
  
## 4. Register Your Knowledge  
  
Record what you learn.  
  
Methods:  
  
- Notes  
- Mind maps  
- Projects  
- Teaching  
  
---  
  
## 5. Write Notes in Your Own Words  
  
Avoid copying.  
  
Explain concepts as if teaching someone else.  
  
---  
  
## 6. Share Your Knowledge  
  
Ways to share:  
  
- Blog posts  
- GitHub  
- LinkedIn  
- Twitter/X  
- Teaching friends  
- YouTube
