## Focus Rule (Don't Overcommit)

You can realistically prepare deeply for **3–4 major hackathons per year**, not all of them.

Choose hackathons aligned with your skills:

- [ ] AI / ML
- [ ] Cloud
- [ ] Space Data
- [ ] Web Innovation

These match the **MERN + AI development path**.
