### 1. LinkedIn (Underrated but powerful)

- Fix your headline → clearly show what you do + business value
- Daily actions:
    - Connect with founders, startups, agencies
    - Post small case studies (proof of work)

👉 Goal: Build visibility + trust

---

### 2. Local Businesses (Fastest way to get clients)

- Target businesses with poor/no websites:
    - Gyms, clinics, restaurants
    - Real estate, coaching centers
    - Salons, lawyers, CA firms

👉 Why this works:

They **need websites but don’t know how to get them**

---

### 3. Upwork / Freelancer (Don’t spam)

- It’s crowded → random applying = waste
- Only apply when:
    - Clear need (website, bug fix, redesign, React dev)
- Always send **custom proposals**

👉 Quality > quantity

---

### 4. Partnerships (Smart scaling move)

- Partner with:
    - Designers
    - SEO freelancers
    - Marketers
    - Agencies

👉 They get clients → you build → both earn

---

## ⚡ 7-Day Execution Plan

### Day 1: Setup

- Portfolio website
- 3 strong projects
- Price sheet (PDF)
- Optimized LinkedIn

👉 Without this → clients don’t trust you

---

### Day 2–7: Outreach (This is the real game)

Daily targets:

- 20 LinkedIn DMs
- 20 Instagram messages
- 10 cold emails
- 5 Upwork proposals

👉 Total = **55 contacts/day**

---

## ⚠️ Reality Check

- Most people fail because:
    - They send 2–3 messages and stop
    - They avoid outreach
    - They don’t show proof

---

## 🧠 Bottom Line

- This is not about skill alone
- It’s a **numbers + consistency game**
