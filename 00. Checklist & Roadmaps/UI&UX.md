# Daily Practice

Every day:

- 30 min UI inspiration (Mobbin, Awwwards, Land-book)
- 1 hr Figma
- 1 hr redesign a real app
- 30 min typography/color/layout
- 30 min UX reading
- 1 hr build the design in React/Tailwind
