**Week 1: Get used to hearing and understanding English**

- ☐ Listen to English news.
- ☐ Read English books and newspapers.
- ☐ Listen to English podcasts, videos, and songs.
- ☐ Shadow the speaker.
- ☐ Sources: Carisma on Command, BBC Learn
- ☐ Learn new English vocabulary.
- ☐ Ensure vocabulary is simple and understandable.

**Week 2: 3-5-5 Challenge**

- ☐ Message 5 people on social media.
- ☐ Create subtitles (source: Descript).
- ☐ Remove filler words.
- ☐ Use pauses effectively.
- ☐ Review mistakes.

**Next Week: Speaking Practice**

- ☐ Go to events and talk to other people.
- ☐ Socialize with people.
- ☐ Speak on stage if possible.
- ☐ Think clearly.

**Next Week: Communication Skills**

- ☐ Email 5 people.
- ☐ Talk with 5 people over the phone. (Source: Grammarly)
- ☐ Practice communicating by phone.
- ☐ Record 5-minute videos of yourself daily, discussing life and goals.
- ☐ Listen to the videos and take notes on:
    - ☐ Way of speaking
        - ☐ Tone
        - ☐ Stammerings
        - ☐ Clarity
        - ☐ Understanding
- ☐ Visual Review:
    - ☐ Body Posture
    - ☐ Hand Movement
    - ☐ Leg movement
