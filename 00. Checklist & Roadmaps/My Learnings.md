# Agar tum mere younger brother hote...

Main tumhe ye plan deta.

**Age 22–25**

- Become an exceptional Product Engineer.
- Master Full Stack.
- Learn AI integration deeply.
- Work in a startup (preferably 20–100 people) or as a founding engineer.
- Build one SaaS on the side.
- Learn marketing by actually getting users, not by watching videos.

**Age 25–28**

- Quit only if your product has real traction.
- Build a company around one successful product.
- Hire slowly.
- Keep ownership.
