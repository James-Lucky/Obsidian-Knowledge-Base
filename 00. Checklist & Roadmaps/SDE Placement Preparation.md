## 1. Resume & LinkedIn

### Maintain Continuously

- Update resume after every **project, achievement, or internship**
- Update **LinkedIn profile** immediately after new work
- Keep resume **always ready**, never build it from scratch
- Apply to new openings **within one week**

### Profile Optimization

- Include only **relevant tech skills**
- Include only **relevant projects**
- Include only **relevant achievements**

### Focused Skill Profile

- Mention **1–2 programming languages for DSA**
- Mention **1–2 development stacks**
- Ensure **projects support the tech stack you list**

# 2. Computer Science Fundamentals

### Core Subjects

- Object-Oriented Programming (OOP)
- Database Management Systems (DBMS)
- Operating Systems (OS)
- Computer Networks (CN)

### Targeted Study

- Research company-specific focus areas Example: VMware → OS concepts

### Aptitude Preparation

- Dedicate **1 week to aptitude practice**

Important for:

- TCS
- Infosys
- Wipro
- Investment banks (Goldman Sachs etc.)

---

# 3. T-Approach for Skill Allocation

Focus deeply on **one core strength** depending on your target companies.

## Startups & Mid-size Companies

Primary Focus

- Advanced **development skills**
- Strong **system building ability**

Secondary Skills

- Basic DSA (Easy / Medium problems)

---

## Big Tech MNCs

Primary Focus

- Strong **DSA**
- Solve **Easy / Medium / Hard problems quickly**

Secondary Skills

- Basic development projects

---

## Service-Based Companies

Balanced Preparation

- Aptitude
- DSA (Easy / Medium)
- Projects
- Communication skills

---

# 4. Company Research Strategy

### Before Applying

- Research interview process
- Study previous interview questions
- Understand required skills

### Resume Customization

- Modify resume for each company

Example

FinTech company → highlight **finance-related projects**

---

# 5. Consistency, Communication & Confidence

### Consistency

- Practice daily across all skills
- Continue preparation until **final offer**

---

### Communication (Spoken English)

- Practice **mock interviews**
- Speak with friends or mentors
- Use AI tools for interview simulation

---

### Confidence

- Only mention skills you **actually know**
- Be able to explain every project on resume
- Treat early rejections as **practice interviews**
