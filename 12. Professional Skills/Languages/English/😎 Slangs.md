## 💬 Chat Abbreviations

| Short Form | Full Form | Meaning |
|------------|-----------|---------|
| BTW | By the way | Used to introduce additional information. |
| TBH | To be honest | Used before giving an honest opinion. |
| BRB | Be right back | Temporarily leaving the conversation. |
| IRL | In real life | Refers to the real world, not online. |
| IDK | I don't know | Expresses uncertainty. |
| SMH | Shaking my head | Expresses disappointment or disbelief. |
| ASAP | As soon as possible | As quickly as possible. |
| DIY | Do it yourself | Build or repair something yourself. |
| FAQ | Frequently Asked Questions | A list of common questions and answers. |
| AKA | Also Known As | Another name for someone or something. |
| FYI | For Your Information | Sharing useful information. |
| LMK | Let me know | Asking someone to inform you. |
| LOL | Laughing Out Loud | Something is funny. |
| LMAO | Laughing My Ass Off | Something is extremely funny. *(Informal)* |
| JK | Just Kidding | Indicates a joke. |
| IDC | I Don't Care | Expresses lack of interest. *(Can sound rude.)* |
