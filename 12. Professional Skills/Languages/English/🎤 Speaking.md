# 1. Tongue Twisters

Practice these daily to improve:
- Pronunciation
- Fluency
- Clarity
- Mouth flexibility
- Speaking confidence

---

# 🟢 Beginner

### 1. Peter Piper ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

> Peter Piper picked a peck of pickled peppers.  
> A peck of pickled peppers Peter Piper picked.

🎯 Focus:
- P sound
- Quick pronunciation

---

### 2. Red Lorry, Yellow Lorry ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

> Red lorry, yellow lorry.

🎯 Focus:
- R and L sounds

---

### 3. Toy Boat ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

> Toy boat.  
> Toy boat.  
> Toy boat.

🎯 Focus:
- T and B sounds

---

### 4. Unique New York ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

> Unique New York.

🎯 Focus:
- Y sound

### 5. Fresh Fried Fish ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

🎯 Focus:
